Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Simply fill out our membership application online. We'll review your application and contact you within 2-3 business days to complete the process.

What are the membership fees?

HURF Chapter offers affordable membership tiers based on business size. Contact us for current pricing and to discuss which tier is right for your business.

Can I attend events if I'm not a member?

Yes! Select networking events and workshops are open to non-members. Check our events page for details. However, members receive priority registration and discounted rates.

Do you help businesses outside the HURF region?

Our primary focus is Hempstead, Uniondale, Roosevelt, and Freeport. However, we welcome businesses from neighboring Nassau County communities that want to connect with the HURF business ecosystem.

How can I get help with MWBE certification?

Our Government Contracting Advocacy program provides step-by-step assistance with MWBE certification. Contact us to schedule a consultation with our certification specialist.

Do you offer one-on-one business consulting?

Yes! Members have access to free consultations with our business advisors on topics like financing, marketing, operations, and growth strategies. Non-members can schedule paid consultations.
